About me:

I am a curious individual who loves solving problems and trying new things. Often in life, this comes with a fear of "diving in". When I was just a toddler, my parents and I were standing on the dock of the family lake cabin when an idea popped into my head. "I think I'm gonna do something," my mom heard me say as I leaped into the late-April water. It was a shock to the system, but I learned a lesson that has stuck with me all my life: the best way to learn something new is to dive in!

Background:

I am originally from good ol' Spokane, Washington and moved to Portland about 5 years ago. Soon after moving to Portland, I was quickly immersed in the music scene. From then on writng and playing music was my main pastime, while I worked full-time as a care provider for adults with special needs.

With COVID-19 throwing everyone for a loop in 2020, I began thinking more and more about my future and how I needed to make a drastic change in my career path. A few friends of mine had completed the full-time course at Epicodus and are now working happily in tech. With their advice and encouragement I decided to go all in and sign up for the program with no prior coding experience. I am beyond excited and hopeful to see where my future in coding will lead me!
